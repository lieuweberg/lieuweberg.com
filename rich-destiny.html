rich-destiny

rich-destiny is a discord rich presence tool for destiny 2

Note: This site is made mostly for Desktop. Please view on there for the best experience (this page may overflow on mobile) and example images.


This tool (software) runs as a Windows service and is thus a background process that starts whenever you start your computer. By not having a GUI directly, but instead communicating with this website, its download size, memory and CPU usage are severely less (~25kB, 10MB and 0.0-0.1%, respectively) compared to that of other software with a similar functionality. Even though it communicates with this site, no data ever leaves your computer. This site is static.
